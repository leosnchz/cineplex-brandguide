Timeplay

Background

Part of the Cineplex Pre-Show, TimePlay is a first of its kind gaming experience that you play before your movie. Users play the game on-screen in theatres by downloading the app and then playing in real time before their movie. Players can win prizes ranging from SCENE points and concession items to prizing provided through sponsorship.
