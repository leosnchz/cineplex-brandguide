introduction

What

The Brand Guidelines document provides information on grid and colour systems, typographic and copy considerations, brand positioning, program logos, and best practices for all of Cineplex’s brands. If there are specific concerns or areas that are not covered in this guide, please contact the design team: CreativeProjects@Cineplex.com

Who

Anyone who will be working with a Cineplex brand designer (in-house and freelancers), marketing, executives, promotional partners, Design and Construction, etc. Being familiar with the Cineplex Brand Guidelines will facilitate clearer communication between all parties and help ensure messaging is streamlined and consistent.

Why

Cineplex is an ever-growing company with a wide range of businesses and brands. To maintain brand consistency across all programs and initiatives, including a streamlined hierarchy of messaging and visual elements, it is essential to adhere to the standardized Brand Guidelines.

While each program has its own look and feel, following these guidelines ensures that a piece is recognizable as being "Cineplex" no matter what it is communicating, or where it is located. Consistent branding leads to an increase in overall brand awareness.

How/When

Clients: Anyone who will be requesting a project should familiarize themselves with the Cineplex Brand Guidelines to help structure (and focus) their main message (see: Communication Framework).

Designers: Anyone who will be working on a project request should be well versed in the Cineplex Brand Guidelines. Clients who request projects may not always be familiar with our branding guidelines, so it is the responsibility of the design team to apply appropriate standards to any project. If unsure about branding, designers should contact their direct manager for clarification and direction.
