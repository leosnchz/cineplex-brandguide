MISSION

Passionately delivering an exceptional entertainment experience.

KEY BRAND BENEFIT

I could use more entertainment in my life (and Cineplex is the best place to get it!)

REASON TO BELIEVE

  • Personal

  • Immersive
  • Indulgent

TARGET CONSUMER

Pleasure Pursuers: people who recognize the power of making time for pleasure, pursue it whenever possible, and seek new ways of keeping leisure on their life list

Pleasure Deprived: people who are slaves to the ‘busyness of life’, have allowed play time lose its proper place, and need to be inspired to put pleasure back on their ‘to do’ lists.

Personality

Brand Essence

Cineplex is your personal emotional escape.

BRAND POSITIONING

Cineplex is a champion for making pleasure a priority in the lives of Canadians.

Consumer Insight

The ‘busyness of life’ takes precedence and the time people spend ‘turning off life’ is diminishing. Canadians have given in to the daily pressures, guilty feelings, and societal norms that diminish the pursuit of fun and make enjoyment less of a priority.

The Result: pleasure has lost its rightful place in people’s lives.

Brand Vision

To be the primary source and force for pleasure accross Canada.

Legal

PRINT

Cineplex legal should appear on all printed materials. Single-sided print materials must have legal showing. On double-sided print materials legal does not have to appear on the front next to the logo as long as it is present within the legal copy on the reverse side.

OUTDOOR SIGNAGE

Legal Marks do not need to be on exterior backlit physical logo signage (ie. diecut Channel Lettering). All other instances of our logos and brands should have their TM/® on the piece.

WEB

Legal is not required on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Tumblr, ROS ads, Cineplex and Cineplex Store rotator banners. However, legal is required on microsites and landing pages so that when people click through on the web ads they have all of the information readily available.

IN-THEATRE DIGITAL SIGNAGE

ATKs, Customer Facing Screens, Digital Box Office Marketing boards, and Merchandisers should have legal displayed in a lower corner.

PRE-SHOW / LOBBY SHOW

Legal should appear in the bottom left corner.

ENGLISH

®/™Cineplex Entertainment LP or used under license.

FRENCH

MD/MCCineplex Divertissement LP ou utilisation sous licence.

BILINGUAL

®/™/MD/MC Cineplex Entertainment LP or used under license/ou utilisation sous licence.

OTHER

The first instance of any brand or product name in a paragraph should have the legal mark. If the Logo appears first, then subsequent uses of the name in text do not need a legal mark. Legal should be on the bottom corner if logo is used.

In advertising pieces, since we have legal marks on our logos placed in the bottom corner of the ad, we generally do not include legal marks on any of the headline/body copy, but it does remain in the legal text at the very end of the document.

Notes

English vs French TM = MC ® = MD
