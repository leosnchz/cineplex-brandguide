PRIME SEATS

BACKGROUND

Cineplex Prime Seats offers larger, wide-back rocker seating that can be reserved in advance – similar to the seating offered throughout our VIP Cinemas. This concept, which was developed in response to overwhelming demand for reserved seating, is in its pilot phase at six Ontario Cineplex theatres.

ABOUT

English: prime seats
French: places de choix URL: Cineplex.com/PrimeSeats

ULTRA AVX

BACKGROUND

An auditorium outfitted for an enhanced Audio Visual Experience featuring immersive sound (Dolby® Atmos in select locations), stunning picture on a massive screen, and reserved seating in larger rocker seats.

While the logo uses all caps, in its written form UltraAVX should be styled with a capital ‘U’ and ‘AVX’, while ‘ltra’ remains lowercase.

ABOUT

English/French: UltraAVX
URL: Cineplex.com/UltraAVX

LEGAL

English: UltraAVX ®
French: UltraAVX MD

MOVIE FIRST ADS / POSTERS

When paired with a movie, UltraAVX ads (print and web) are treated as any other ad. Hero artwork in one section, with the information in the other – with the UltraAVX and Cineplex logos side by side separated by a line.

EXTERIOR BANNERS

Typically the UltraAVX and Cineplex logos would sit side by side, but to emphasize the UltraAVX logo on the Exterior Banners (for size/legibility/impact at a distance), we opt to use the Cineplex branded version of the logo.
