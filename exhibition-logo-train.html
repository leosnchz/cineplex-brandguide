CINEPLEX LOGO TRAIN LOCKUP - ENGLISH

ENGLISH

The English Logo Train Lockup is comprised of the Cineplex logo, and our English Theatre brand logos. The lockup is often used for corporate products (on the backs of gift cards and gift card 3rd party backers), coupons, and in presentations, directed at English-only markets.

HOW TO USE

The logo train is available in six colour variations: Full Colour (4C), Full Colour Knock Out (4CKO), Blue, Dark Silver, Black (K), and White/Knock Out (KO).

The rings should not appear on any of the sub brands if the masterbrand with rings is present.

Like any other logo placement, this lockup should either be centered, or aligned to the right, positioned at the bottom of artwork.

MINIMUM SIZE
