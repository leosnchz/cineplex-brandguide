Primary Brand Colours

Our colour palette is driven by a bold and bright Blue and Gold.

Blue signifies confidence, intelligence, and dependability, while Gold embodies creativity, playfulness and emotion.

The Consumer Blue and Gold are complimented by Black and Light Silver and can be leveraged for a variety of different touch points and executions.

Black and Light Silver are applicable to any communication we create. The use of Corporate and Consumer Blues (when and where to use them) are outlined as follows:

AUDIENCE: CORPORATE

Corporate colours should only be used in B2B environments. Use Light Silver rings in logos (Cineplex Inc., Cineplex Entertainment, etc.), when applicable, in colour ads.

AUDIENCE: CONSUMER

Consumer colours should only be used in guest facing environments; within our theatres, direct to guest mail, or out of home messaging. Use Gold rings, when applicable, in colour ads.

Gradient Guidelines

When setting up gradients, use three markers. One at 0% (main/predominant colour), 70% (slightly adjusted), and 100% (slightly adjusted further).

Predominently use radial gradients, especially for backgrounds. When using gradients, the idea is that the main/predominant colour is what is seen the most (brighter in the center), while darker colours should remain closer to the edges of the working/ad space.
