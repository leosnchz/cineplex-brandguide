CINEPLEX VIP

BACKGROUND

With VIP Cinemas presented by Scotiabank, guests enjoy a premium entertainment experience available exclusively at Cineplex Entertainment theatres in Canada. They feature specially designed auditoriums exclusively for adult guests, in-seat menu service, a licensed lounge and a dedicated box office. VIP auditoriums feature reserved seating enabling guests to select their specific seat in advance.

A VIP auditorium also features full stadium seating with larger, luxurious seats with tabletops embedded in their armrest. Ordered items will be delivered directly to the guest at their seat. Guests can also enjoy the licensed lounge before and after a movie.

ABOUT

English:

presented by Scotiabank

French:
Cinéma VIP Cineplex présenté par Banque Scotia

COLOURS

VIP Cinemas artwork can be indentified by its use of Black, Gold and signature Teal accent colour from the Cineplex Colour Pallette.

ADVERTISEMENTS

Magazine ads/posters are used to highlight photography with a headline (often in teal), body copy and CTA. They do not require the diamond divider as the colour variation in the headline helps break up the text.

OPERATIONAL SIGNAGE

Operational signs are text-only, but they do pick up the diamond horizontal divider as a way to break up text (which generally stays all white), and helps to reinforce VIP colours (black, gold, teal) and branding.
