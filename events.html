EVENTS

BACKGROUND

Cineplex Events (formerly Front Row Centre Events) bring world class entertainment to local theatres. Presented in HD with digital surround sound, guests can experience the best in original one-night only and series-based programming (Met Opera Live in HD, Broadway productions, London’s National Theatre broadcast live direct from the stage, dance performances, classic films, concerts, eSports, live sporting events and documentaries).

URLs

The Events URL should be used when an ad combines various programs into one piece. Otherwise, one of the following URLs should be used where appropriate for each ad:

Programs: National Theatre Live, Branagh Theatre Live, Stratford Festival HD
URL: Cineplex.com/Stage

Program: The Metropolitan Opera HD Live
URL: Cineplex.com/MetOpera

Programs: In The Gallery
URL: Cineplex.com/InTheGallery

URL: Cineplex.com/Events

Classic Films

BACKGROUND

A great opportunity for classic film fans to see their favourites in digital on the big screen! A series of classic film titles will be screened in a digital format. The intent is to showcase classic films that, in most cases, have not been seen on the big screen in this format before.

ABOUT

English: Classic Films
URL: Cineplex.com/ClassicFilms

French: Classiques du Cinéma
URL: Cineplex.com/ClassiquesDuCinema

FAMILY FAVOURITES

BACKGROUND

Join us on Saturday mornings at 11am for Cineplex Family Favourites. Watch a family favourite film each week for only $2.99.

A portion of the proceeds goes towards supporting Cineplex’s charitable partner WE (formerly Free The Children). It’s a fun and affordable way to enjoy the weekend with your family!

ABOUT

English: Family Favourites
URL: Cineplex.com/Family

French: Les Films en Famille
URL: Cineplex.com/Famille

POPCORN PARTIES

BACKGROUND

Children’s birthday parties (13 years of age and younger). Movie, Kid’s Tray Combo, Party Host, reserved theatre seating, and a gift for the birthday child.

ABOUT

English: Popcorn Parties URL: Cineplex.com/Theatres/Birthdays

STARS & STROLLERS

BACKGROUND

Cineplex Entertainment’s Stars and Strollers program provides new parents the opportunity to experience Hollywood’s latest movies with their children. The program offers a baby-friendly environment, which allows guests to attend a movie without fear that disruption caused by young children will disturb other guests.

PROGRAM PARTNER

The promotional partner (currently Toys’R’Us, Babies’R’Us) has its logo knocked out within the ribbon portion of the logo.

ABOUT

English: Stars & Strollers
URL: Cineplex.com/StarsAndStrollers

French: Vedettes et Poussettes
URL: Cineplex.com/VedettesEtPoussettes

SENSORY FRIENDLY SCREENINGS

BACKGROUND

This program provides a sensory friendly environment for individuals with autism spectrum disorder or anyone for whom the environment is suitable and their families to view new release films in theatres across the country. Films are presented with lights up and sound turned down.

ABOUT

English: Sensory Friendly Screenings in partnership with Autism Speaks Canada
URL: Cineplex.com/SensoryFriendly

French: Représentations Adaptées aux Sensibilités Sensorielles en partenariat avec Autism Speaks Canada
URL: Cineplex.com/Cinemas/SensibilitesSensorielles
