CINEPLEX STORE

BACKGROUND

The Cineplex Store (CineplexStore.com) is Canada’s one-stop shop for digital movie rentals and downloads.

With more than 7,000 titles, CineplexStore.com offers Canada’s largest selection of UltraViolet movie downloads. Cineplex Store customers can also earn and redeem points with SCENE, Canada’s largest entertainment loyalty program.

SUPERTICKET

BACKGROUND

SuperTicket is the world’s first-ever bundled offering from multiple studios that allows movie-goers to purchase a movie admission ticket and pre-order the UltraViolet digital download of a movie at the same time.

ABOUT

English: SuperTicket
URL: Cineplex.com/SuperTicket

Assets

SuperTicket has a regular rotation of web assets (ROS ads, Facebook, Twitter, Cineplex and Store rotator banners), a minimal exterior banner, and a short-run campaign with movie posters (with Pugs) and buttons for cast members.
